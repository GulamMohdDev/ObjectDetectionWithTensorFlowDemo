# Privacy Policy for Object Detection Demo

**Last Updated: October 26, 2023**

This Privacy Policy describes how your information is handled in the "Object Detection Demo" application.

### 1. Overview
Object Detection Demo is a learning-oriented application designed to demonstrate object detection and classification using TensorFlow Lite on Android. This app is intended for educational and demonstration purposes only.

### 2. Information Collection and Use
We do not collect, store, or share any personal information. 

#### Camera Access
The app requires access to your device's camera to function. The camera stream is used solely for real-time object detection and image classification. 
- **Local Processing:** All image processing and machine learning inference are performed locally on your device.
- **No Data Storage:** No images, videos, or metadata from the camera are saved to your device's storage or transmitted to any external servers.

### 3. Third-Party Services
The app uses **TensorFlow Lite**, an open-source deep learning framework by Google, to perform on-device inference. TensorFlow Lite does not collect any personally identifiable information from your device through this app.

### 4. Security
Since the app does not collect or transmit any data, your privacy is maintained by the local nature of the application. However, please be aware that no method of electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure.

### 5. Children's Privacy
This app does not collect any information from children or any other users.

### 6. Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes.

### 7. Contact Us
If you have any questions or suggestions about this Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact the developer.
